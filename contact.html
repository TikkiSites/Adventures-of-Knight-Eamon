Welcome Authors!

Adventure, fantasy, children's stories. I love a variety of stories, and I look forward to illustrating for you. Thank you for viewing my art and for your interest in my illustrations. Please, provide your contact information* and fill out this form to give me a summary of your story and the type of illustrations you want**. When you complete the form, it will be sent to my email. *I will not sell or give anyone else your information.

**Please note that I do not illustrate anything sexual, immodest, or inappropriate. I keep my art clean and wholesome. I thank you for your respect in honoring my illustrative decisions.
