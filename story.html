Story Summary

The story on this website is about a young knight that is the guardian of a princess. Come read their story and connect with them as they set out on many adventures. Meet new characters and travel to distant lands. Enjoy this developing story, filled with mystery, adventure, and — eventually — a little romance. Enjoy the imagery of the story through illustrations and storyboards.

Development

I am building the story as I go along. There are a few hopes I have for my characters, but much of the story has not been developed yet. I will be learning more about the characters along with my readers. I thought this would be a fun way to build a story. As I complete each chapter, I will add them to this website. To receive notifications of Knight Eamon’s next adventures, you can follow me on FaceBook or Tumblr, where I will post when I add new chapters.
